# Voice & Conversational AI UX

- Voice interface design principles
  - Persona design (personality through voice)
  - Speech evaluates character instantly
  - Trust through voice attributes
  - Brand representation through sound
- Conversational mechanics
  - Move conversation forward (Maxim of Quantity)
  - Brief and relevant responses
  - Context leverage (dialog history awareness)
  - Word order and stress for focus
- Natural speech patterns
  - Don't teach "commands" - speech is intuitive
  - Avoid touchtone-era UI patterns
  - Questions, not instructions
  - Conversational reprompts vs. robotic errors
- Voice-specific error handling
  - Misrecognition recovery
  - Gentle reprompts
  - Context-preserving corrections
  - Natural repair sequences
